High Blood Pressure with Diabetes

Diabetes and high blood pressure together are responsible for about 70% of kidney failure cases. Thus, prevention of diabetes and high blood pressure will go a long way in decreasing the burden of kidney patients in the community. A healthy diet, regular exercise, regular medical examination and appropriate medicines as prescribed by your physician can help in controlling diabetes and high blood pressure and prevent kidney problems. - Dr. Sonia Yaqub, Assistant Professor of Medicine
